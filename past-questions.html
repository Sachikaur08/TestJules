(CBSE 2020) Two equal and opposite charges of magnitude 2 x 10^-7 C are placed 15 cm apart. What is the magnitude and direction of the electric field at the midpoint of the line joining the two charges?

The electric field at the midpoint is 6.4 x 10^5 N/C, directed from the positive charge to the negative charge.

(CBSE 2019) A hollow metallic sphere of radius 10 cm is charged such that the potential of its surface is 80 V. The potential at the centre of the sphere is:

The potential at the centre of the sphere is 80 V, as the potential inside a hollow metallic sphere is the same as on the surface.

(CBSE 2018) Two point charges +q and -2q are placed at the vertices 'B' and 'C' of an equilateral triangle ABC of side 'a'. The magnitude of the resultant electric field at the vertex A is:

The magnitude of the resultant electric field at the vertex A is (kq^2)/a^2 * sqrt(3).
